Technical Publications Team Lead

Ottawa, ON./ Victoria, B.C./ Halifax, N.S., (CAN.) - Hybrid

Position Summary

Thales is looking for a Technical Publications Team Lead is a high-level individual contributor role designed to facilitate the day-to-day coordination of the Technical Data Package (TDP) lifecycle. Reporting directly to the Technical Documentation Manager, this role is essential to supporting the Manager by providing consistent workflow oversight, technical review, and process coordination within a complex defence environment.

The Team Lead acts as a functional link between the Manager and the project teams, ensuring that documentation tasks stay on schedule and adhere to required standards. This position is dedicated to the functional execution of documentation processes and quality assurance, serving as a key pillar of support for the Technical Documentation Manager in maintaining the integrity and delivery of all technical data products.

Regulatory Compliance Requirement

This position requires direct or indirect access to hardware, software or technical information controlled under the Canadian Export Control List and/or the US the Export Administration Regulations (EAR). All applicants must be eligible or able to obtain authorization for such access.

Key Areas of Responsibility 

Workflow Coordination & Management Support
Operational Triage: Assist the Manager by triaging daily Document Change Package (DCP/r) requests and Problem Incident Reports (PIR), ensuring they are appropriately tracked and progressed through the lifecycle.
Daily Workflow Oversight: Monitor the progress of tasks assigned to staff and contractors to ensure delivery timelines for major programmes are met, escalating potential bottlenecks to the Manager.
Reporting & Visibility: Maintain and update Dashboards and KPIs to provide the Manager with a clear, real-time view of technical data package status and team productivity.
Documentation Standardisation: Develop and maintain work instructions, style guides, and instructional materials to assist the team in producing consistent, high-quality output.
Technical Review & Quality Assurance
TDP Gatekeeping: Perform thorough peer reviews and technical checks on various publications (CFTOs, LSARs, BOMs, and drawings) to ensure compliance with military and customer standards before final approval.
Change Package Processing: Review and prepare disposition recommendations for Document Change Packages, liaising with technical contributors to address requirements and coordinate revisions.
Audit & Review Support: Assist the Manager in preparing for Physical Configuration Audits (PCA) and design reviews by organising evidence, tracking progress, and ensuring the final TDP is logically structured.
Verification Coordination: Track and report on the status of technical verification activities to ensure all products are accurate and compliant with the support solution.
Systems & Process Support
Workflow Administration: Oversee the day-to-day operation of the Master Data Management Portal, using SharePoint and Nintex to process deliveries to external stakeholders and clients.
Process Enhancement: Identify manual bottlenecks and assist the Manager in implementing automated solutions using VBA, SQL, or XML to improve team efficiency.
Tool Maintenance: Serve as the primary functional user for the OmegaPS suite and related logistics tools, identifying bugs and coordinating resolutions with vendors to ensure system availability.
ILS & Stakeholder Liaison
Cross-Functional Alignment: Coordinate with Obsolescence, Sparing, and Engineering teams to ensure that publication updates are synchronised with Integrated Logistics Support (ILS) functions and LSAR entries.
Data Indexing: Facilitate the indexing of documentation in accordance with the National Defence Index of Documentation (NDID) and the relevant Equipment Family Tree (EFT).


Minimum Qualifications

Education: Minimum 2-year technical programme diploma from a recognised institute in a related technical field (Mechanical, Electrical, Software, or Naval Architecture) or eligibility for registration as a technician.
Experience: * Minimum of 5 years of demonstrated experience in the production, revision, and coordination of complex Technical Data Packages (TDP).
Proven experience in a senior analyst or coordinator role within a defence, marine, or aerospace environment.
Technical Knowledge; Comprehensive understanding of Integrated Logistics Support (ILS) and LSAR management; Advanced proficiency in OmegaPS, IPESA, SharePoint, and Nintex Workflow;Strong ability to interpret engineering drawings, technical manuals, and Bills of Materials (BOM).
Specialised Skills: Functional familiarity with SQL, XML, and VBA to support documentation management.
Compliance: Direct experience navigating military and Department of National Defence (DND) regulations and standards.
